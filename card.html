Cat

Did you know that newborn kittens are blind, deaf and completely reliant on their mother!?

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Litter Size: 2-5 kittens
  • Average Jump Height: 7x their height
  • Running Speed: up to 30 mph

Cats are small domesticated mammals. "Kitten" is derived from Old French and means "little cat". A group of kittens is refered to as a kindle and a group of cats is refered to as a clowder.
