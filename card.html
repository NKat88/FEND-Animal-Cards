Kitten

Did you know that newborn kittens are blind, deaf and completely reliant on their mother!?

  • Kingdom/Class/Family: Animalia/Mammalia/Felidae
  • Litter Size: 2-5 kittens
  • Age they can leave their mother: 8 - 12 weeks
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years

Kittens come in many different colors. This kitten is adorable with it's light blue eyes and calico coloring. "Kitten" is derived from Old French and means "little cat". It takes a kitten roughly a year to become a cat.
